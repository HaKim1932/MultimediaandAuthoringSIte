About This Site

Context

This website serves as a digital portfolio for the Multimedia and Authoring course, documenting the creative journey through two major projects: Rotoscoping and 3D Rendering. Each project challenged the application of digital storytelling tools and techniques across multiple weeks of work.

The rotoscoping project involved splitting reference footage into 5-second clips, rendering them through After Effects, then tracing and editing each frame in Photoshop to produce a stylized animation from live-action movement.

The 3D rendering project focused on building a realistic model of the solar system using industry-standard tools. It explores concepts like lighting, texturing, orbital mechanics, and perspective — all rendered to produce a visually compelling output.

Together, these projects reflect a growing proficiency in digital media authoring and a genuine curiosity for the intersection of art and technology.
